Privacy Policy – Simple CRM

Last updated: May 10, 2024

Simple CRM (“the extension”) is a Gmail-native CRM tool designed to help users manage contacts, track opportunities, and run mail merge campaigns using Google Sheets and the Google Apps Script Mail service.

We take privacy seriously and collect only the minimum data required for the extension to function.

Email processing and CRM functionality are designed to run locally in your browser. We do not operate a backend server for storing or processing your email data via external AI APIs.

1. Data We Access (Google OAuth)

The extension uses Google OAuth to access specific data to provide its core services. We adhere to the principle of "Least Privilege," accessing only what is necessary:

  • Google Sheets (.../auth/spreadsheets): Used to read and write CRM data and campaign results within spreadsheets you designate.
  • Google Drive (.../auth/drive.file): Used strictly to create and edit specific spreadsheet files created by the extension.
  • User Identity (.../auth/userinfo.email): Used to identify your account for subscription management and trial tracking.
  • Google Apps Script Mail Service (.../auth/script.send_mail): Used solely to execute mail merge campaigns that you manually trigger. This allows the extension to send emails via Apps Script without needing access to your full Gmail inbox history.

2. Gmail Integration & "Email Surgery"

The extension runs inside your Gmail browser tab to help associate open email threads with CRM records and provide productivity features.

  • Selective Access: The extension only reads the sender email address, subject, and visible message text of the thread currently open on your screen when you manually interact with the extension.
  • Local Processing: The "Email Surgery" feature uses rule-based logic to strip signatures, disclaimers, and junk text to create concise CRM notes. This processing is performed locally within your browser; email content is never transmitted to our servers or third-party AI APIs.
  • Task Suggestions: The extension may scan the active thread for keywords (e.g., "meeting", "pricing") or time-based phrases (e.g., "next week") to suggest follow-up actions. This analysis happens entirely on your device.
  • User-Triggered: Content is only accessed when you view an email or click “Log email”, and is not accessed in the background for unrelated purposes.

3. Google API Limited Use Disclosure

Simple CRM's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We do NOT:

  • Use Google user data for advertising.
  • Sell or share user data with third-party data brokers.
  • Use email content for training AI models or any purpose outside the core CRM note-logging functionality.

4. Data Storage

  • Locally (on your device): The extension stores CRM metadata and duplicate protection hashes using chrome.storage.local. This data remains on your device.
  • Email Summaries & Notes: Any summaries generated via the "Email Surgery" feature are stored locally in your browser and then transmitted directly to your designated Google Sheet. They are never stored on our servers.
  • On Google Sheets: Your main CRM data remains within the specific Google Sheets files that you control.

5. Mail Merge Sending Mechanism

Mail merge campaigns are handled via a "Private Bridge" using Google Apps Script.

  • Sending is only initiated when you explicitly click "Send" or "Test Send."
  • The extension does not require access to your full Gmail inbox history through the Gmail API. It cannot search your inbox, delete messages, or modify settings.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not operate a backend server for storing your CRM or email data. We do not use third-party AI APIs to process your email content. Data is only exchanged with Google services via encrypted HTTPS connections when you explicitly trigger a feature.

7. Payment Processing

We use ExtensionPay and Stripe to manage subscriptions and process payments.

  • Personal Data: When you subscribe, ExtensionPay stores your email address and payment status.
  • Financial Data: We never see or store your credit card details. All transactions are handled securely by Stripe.
  • For more details, see the ExtensionPay Privacy Policy.

8. Export and Deletion

  • Export: You can export your CRM data at any time via your associated Google Sheets.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the extension removes locally stored metadata. Any Google Sheets must be manually deleted from your Google Drive.

9. Security

The extension uses Google-approved OAuth2 protocols and Chrome’s secure storage APIs. All communication with Google services is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS.
