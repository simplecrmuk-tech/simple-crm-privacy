Privacy Policy – Simple CRM

Last updated: April 29, 2026

Simple CRM (“the extension”) is a Gmail-native CRM tool designed to help users manage contacts, track opportunities, and run mail merge campaigns using Google Sheets and the Google Apps Script Mail service.

We take privacy seriously and collect only the minimum data required for the extension to function.

Email processing and CRM functionality are designed to run locally in your browser wherever possible. We do not operate a backend server for storing user email data.

1. Data We Access (Google OAuth)

The extension uses Google OAuth to access specific data to provide its core services. We adhere to the principle of "Least Privilege," accessing only what is necessary:

  • Google Sheets (.../auth/spreadsheets): Used to read and write CRM data and campaign results within spreadsheets you designate.
  • Google Drive (.../auth/drive.file): Used strictly to create and edit specific spreadsheet files created by the extension.
  • User Identity (.../auth/userinfo.email): Used to identify your account for subscription management and trial tracking.
  • Google Apps Script Mail Service (.../auth/script.send_mail): Used solely to execute mail merge campaigns that you manually trigger. This allows the extension to send emails via Apps Script without needing access to your full Gmail inbox.

2. Gmail (In-Page Context)

The extension runs inside your Gmail browser tab to help associate open email threads with CRM records and provide productivity features.

  • The extension may read limited Gmail page context, including the sender email address, email subject, and visible email content, solely to support CRM functionality.
  • This includes generating summaries of email conversations and suggesting follow-up actions such as reminders or tasks.
  • Email content is processed locally within your browser to generate summaries and suggestions. This data is not transmitted to any external servers.
  • Email content is only used when you interact with the extension, such as viewing an email or clicking “Log email”, and is not accessed in the background for unrelated purposes.

Email Content Processing

When using Gmail integration features, the extension may process the content of emails currently visible in your Gmail interface to:

  • Generate concise summaries of email conversations.
  • Suggest follow-up actions such as meetings, replies, or reminders.
  • Allow you to save relevant notes to your CRM records.

All email content processing is performed locally within your browser. Email content is not transmitted, stored externally, sold, or shared with any third parties.

3. Google API Limited Use Disclosure

Simple CRM's use and transfer to any other app of information received from Google APIs will adhere to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

We do NOT:

  • Use Google user data for advertising.
  • Sell or share user data with third-party data brokers.
  • Use data for any purpose outside the core CRM and mail merge functionality.

4. Data Storage

  • Locally (on your device): The extension stores CRM data and metadata, including contacts, opportunity records, pipeline stages, notes, and follow-up information, using chrome.storage.local. This data remains on your device and is never sent to our servers.
  • Email Summaries & Notes: Any summaries or notes generated from email content are stored locally in your browser as part of your CRM data and are never transmitted to external servers.
  • On Google Sheets: Your mail merge campaign data, such as recipient lists and send status, is stored within the specific Google Sheets files that you control.

5. Mail Merge Sending Mechanism

Mail merge campaigns are handled via a "Private Bridge" using Google Apps Script.

  • Sending is only initiated when you explicitly click "Send" or "Test Send."
  • Because we use the script.send_mail scope, the extension does not require access to your full Gmail inbox through the Gmail API.
  • The extension cannot search your inbox, delete messages, or modify your Gmail messages through the Gmail API.
  • Mail merge content is only sent when you manually trigger a mail merge action.

6. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not operate a backend server for storing your CRM or email data and do not use third-party analytics or advertising trackers. Data is only exchanged with Google services via encrypted HTTPS connections when you explicitly trigger a feature that requires it, such as creating a Google Sheet or sending a mail merge campaign.

7. Payment Processing

We use ExtensionPay and Stripe to manage subscriptions and process payments.

  • Personal Data: When you subscribe, ExtensionPay stores your email address and payment status to manage your access to Pro features.
  • Financial Data: We do not host a backend and never see or store your credit card details. All payment transactions are handled securely by Stripe, a PCI-compliant payment processor.
  • For more details, see the ExtensionPay Privacy Policy and the Stripe Privacy Policy.

8. Export and Deletion

  • Export: You can export your CRM data at any time via your associated Google Sheets.
  • Deletion: Uninstalling the extension removes locally stored metadata from the browser. Any Google Sheets created by or used with the extension must be manually deleted from your Google Drive if you no longer want them.

9. Security

The extension uses Google-approved OAuth2 protocols and Chrome’s secure storage APIs. All communication with Google services is encrypted using industry-standard HTTPS.
